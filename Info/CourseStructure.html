Course Structure

We will meet once each week in class. The class will be a mix of new materials and classroom exercises. Most weeks will specify reading that you should do before class to get the most from the lecture.

There will be 9 assignments, a mix of one week and two weeks programming exercises. After each submission you will have a code walkthrough with a Professor and TA. This enables you to explain your design and code.

Your assignments will be judged on:

  • Correctness.
  • Quality of design.
  • Appropriate use of tools and techniques.
  • Your ability to explain your program and design decisions.
